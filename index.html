Work Portfolio

Below is a list projects and experiences that have defined my path at JMU

Neustar, Inc. Data Center and DDoS Protection Intern

I learned how to perform network mitigations against cybersecurity threats and deploying applications on Kubernetes

Aerial Multispectral Imaging Capstone

My 2 Year Capstone project consisting of an 8 member team following an agile framework centered on precision agriculture, remote sensing, and web development.

NAE Grand Challenges Scholars Program

An international engineering program whose framework forced me to develop my innovation and entrepreneurial thinking skills to address today's vexing global grand challenges

InnovationX JMU Bluestone Hackathon 2nd Runner-up

A 24 hour transportation design competition sponsored by VDOT where a team of 4 engineers developed a novel approach to tackling the pain points of commuting in a crammed college town

Madison Engineering xChange Junior Winner

JMU's annual public engineering showcase featuring all projects from freshmen to seniors featuring presentations, posters, and prototypes. Our capstone exhibited the best engineering work by the attendees

Think Wrong Blitz Hackathon

A new way of approaching engineering design, Think wrong blitz reshaped my prespective on innovation and diversity by "thinking wrong"

Human Powered Vehicle

Designed, built, and prototyped a human powered vehicle that promised more independence and freedom to a young girl with Arthrogryposis and blindness

Adaptable Musical Instrument

Designed, modeled, 3D Printed, prototyped, and iterated a universal solution for a young boy with limited dexterity to fulfill his aspiration to participate with his classmates and play the xylophone

Bioprocess Engineering Research

Labratory experiments on the pyrolosis of hemp that focuses on the chemical makeup of the biomass and it's potential value as an energy source or animal feed after the extraction of THC and CBD.
